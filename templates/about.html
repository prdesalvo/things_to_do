Get recommendations for things to do from recent and upcoming wedding websites.

The recommendations on this site come from web scrapping the Things to do page on upcoming weddings hosted on The Knot. When you search for a city, the app does a Google search for any upcoming weddings taking place in that area, finds the Things to do page, and then return the results from up to 10 websites.
